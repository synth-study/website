
For authors:

Reduce revise-and-resubmit pain. Catch missing guideline items before you submit.

Real-time feedback shows you exactly what's needed. Stop under-reporting... and stop over-explaining!

Bullet-proof your methods. Ensure guideline items are clearly present and findable, no more "unclear methods" or "insufficient detail".

Better habits with less effort. Guideline compliance becomes automatic and unobtrusive, rather than another task to remember.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

For reviewers:

Rapid triage of submissions: get an instant view of which guideline items are satisfied, missing, or unclear, so you can focus attention on real issues rather than checklist policing.

Consistent, defensible reviews: base your comments on transparent, standardised guideline coverage, making it easier to justify decisions and reduce back-and-forth with authors and editors.
